To experience the prize:

  1. Choose the mode you want to work in
  2. Enter the starting/ending point(s) as required by your mode
  3. Start walking!
  4. When you have walked enough steps, claim your prize!

Please keep in mind social distancing precautions:

  • Maintain six feet of distance between yourself and others
  • Avoid crowded areas
  • Wear a mask or face covering
  • Stay safe and have fun!
